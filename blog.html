Green Ammonia

Now you may be wondering, what's Green Ammonia? Why isn't normal Ammonia sufficient?? The thing is, Ammonia production is one of the largest sources of greenhouse gas emissions – the process uses 1.8% of the world’s energy consumption and is responsible for 1% of total greenhouse gas emissions! As ammonia production is so important and due to its current environmental efficiency, a new cleaner method of producing ammonia must be developed. Surely, we can improve the process!! And that is what Green Ammonia tries to do.
